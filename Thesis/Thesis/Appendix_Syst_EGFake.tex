\chapter{Systematic Variations for Electron to Photon Fake Rate Calculation}
\label{sec:EGFake_appendix}

This appendix shows variation plots for the systematic uncertainty estimation on the fake rates for photons faked by electrons using various $m_{ll}$ invariant mass windows around the Z boson mass (5 GeV, 15 GeV, and 20 GeV window sizes).  From values and deviations from the nominal, a systematic uncertainty was estimated which is then applied to events with a photon that is actually a truth electron.
